About Ustaaz Khaleefah

Ustaaz Khaleefah was a distinguished Islamic scholar and spiritual leader, born into a lineage of great scholars and Imams. He received his Islamic education under the guidance of his father, Maulana Shaykh Imam Umar Mahmood (RTA), and dedicated his entire life to teaching the Qur'an, Fiqh, and the sciences of the Deen — with a special devotion to uplifting souls to the highest spiritual realm and providing them with a solid foundation in Islamic knowledge.

Through his teachings, counsel, and spiritual guidance, there comes this institution — rooted in compassion, discipline, and sincerity. His legacy lives on through this Islamic Institute, guiding students from their very first letters of the Arabic alphabet to advanced studies in Fiqh, Qur'anic recitation, and spiritual healing.
